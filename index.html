What is Pre-Foreclosure?

Pre-foreclosure is the stage of the foreclosure process where the homeowner has missed one or more mortgage payments and is at risk of being foreclosed on. The lender will typically send a notice to the homeowner informing them that they are in default and giving them a certain amount of time to bring their mortgage current. If the homeowners don't bring their mortgage current within that time frame, the lender can begin the foreclosure process.
